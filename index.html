About us

Building the future of tactical textiles in Africa

Anbessa Elite Tactical Textile Industries PLC is an Israeli company that signed an agreement in January 2023 to establish a state-of-the-art textile and apparel manufacturing facility in the Bahir Dar Industrial Park, Ethiopia.

The company is investing USD 3 million and is expected to create employment opportunities for over 1,000 people when fully operational. Our goal is to produce high-quality tactical and camouflage gear using state-of-the-art technology and skilled staff, with dedicated training programs for employees.

Bahir Dar Industrial Park is situated on 75 hectares in Bahir Dar city, 560 km from Addis Ababa. The park became operational in 2020 and specializes in apparel and textile production as a Special Economic Zone. We are proud to contribute to Ethiopia's manufacturing sector and to serve Military and SOF with gear tailored to operational requirements.

Partnership & leadership

The agreement was signed by Aklilu Tadese (CEO of Industrial Parks Development Corporation) and Leo Isaias (owner and manager of Anbessa Elite). Our operations are led by Lior Esayas.
