About us

Building the future of tactical textiles in Africa

Anbesa Elite Tactical Textile Industries PLC is the first of its kind in Africa — a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility specializing in uniforms, tactical gear, and camouflage solutions for security forces and professional organizations worldwide.

Our advanced manufacturing facility in Bahir Dar Industrial Park, Ethiopia spans more than 5,500 square meters and delivers end-to-end solutions from design to full-scale production.

We set a new benchmark in the tactical textile industry by combining cutting-edge technology, expert craftsmanship, and real operational experience.

Our Core Principles

Premium Materials – carefully selected fabrics engineered to withstand demanding operational environments.

Innovative Design – combining functionality, comfort, and advanced technology.

Proven Field Experience – products tested in real operational environments.

International Standards – manufacturing aligned with strict global defense and security requirements.

Our Story

Born from tactical experience

Founded by former Ethiopian officers and soldiers from elite Israeli military units, Anbesa Elite was born from a deep connection to Ethiopia, Africa, and a shared commitment to tactical excellence.

Through our strategic partnership with Spectral-X, we combine advanced Israeli expertise with Ethiopian craftsmanship to create next-generation protective solutions.

Diversity is at the heart of our culture — women make up half of our management team, reflecting our belief that strength comes from inclusion.
